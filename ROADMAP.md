# SubTracker Evolution Roadmap

## Current State
SubTracker is a subscription management tool that visualizes recurring expenses on a calendar with features for adding, editing, importing/exporting, and tracking monthly costs.

---

## Phase 1: Enhanced Core Features

### 1.1 Notification System
- **Renewal Reminders**: Email/browser notifications before subscription renewals
- **Price Change Alerts**: Track and notify when subscription prices increase
- **Trial Period Warnings**: Alert users before free trials end
- **Customizable Timing**: Set notification preferences (7 days, 3 days, 1 day before)

### 1.2 Categories & Tags
- **Category System**: Group subscriptions (Entertainment, Productivity, Fitness, etc.)
- **Custom Tags**: Add flexible tags for better organization
- **Category Analytics**: See spending breakdown by category
- **Color-by-Category**: Auto-assign colors based on categories
- **Filter Views**: Filter calendar and list by category/tag

### 1.3 Payment Methods
- **Track Payment Sources**: Credit card, PayPal, bank account, etc.
- **Card Expiration Warnings**: Alert before payment method expires
- **Payment Analytics**: See which cards are used most
- **Multiple Currencies**: Support international subscriptions with conversion

---

## Phase 2: Advanced Analytics & Insights

### 2.1 Financial Dashboard
- **Spending Trends**: Graph showing spending over time
- **Year-over-Year Comparison**: Compare current vs previous year spending
- **Projected Annual Cost**: Calculate full-year expenses
- **Savings Calculator**: Show potential savings if subscriptions are cancelled
- **Budget Goals**: Set monthly/annual budget limits with progress tracking

### 2.2 Usage Tracking
- **Frequency Logging**: Manually track how often you use each service
- **Value Analysis**: Calculate cost-per-use for each subscription
- **Unused Subscription Detection**: Flag services not used in X days
- **ROI Insights**: Highlight subscriptions that may not be worth keeping

### 2.3 Price History
- **Track Price Changes**: Maintain history of price increases/decreases
- **Price Trends**: Visualize how costs have changed over time
- **Total Savings/Losses**: Show cumulative impact of price changes
- **Competitor Comparison**: Suggest cheaper alternatives

---

## Phase 3: Smart Features & Automation

### 3.1 AI-Powered Suggestions
- **Duplicate Detection**: Identify similar subscriptions (Spotify + Apple Music)
- **Bundle Recommendations**: Suggest money-saving bundles
- **Cancellation Suggestions**: Recommend underused subscriptions to cancel
- **Renewal Optimization**: Suggest switching to annual plans for savings
- **Smart Categorization**: Auto-categorize based on service name

### 3.2 Integration & Automation
- **Bank Account Sync**: Auto-detect subscription charges from transactions
- **Email Parsing**: Scan inbox for subscription receipts and renewals
- **Calendar Integration**: Sync with Google/Outlook calendars
- **API Connections**: Direct integration with popular services
- **IFTTT/Zapier Support**: Trigger actions based on subscription events

### 3.3 Receipt & Document Management
- **Attachment Upload**: Store receipts, contracts, cancellation policies
- **OCR Scanning**: Extract subscription details from receipts
- **Document Search**: Full-text search across all documents
- **Cloud Storage Integration**: Connect Dropbox, Google Drive, etc.

## Phase 4: Collaboration & Sharing

### 4.1 Family/Household Plans
- **Shared Subscriptions**: Track who uses which subscriptions
- **Split Costs**: Calculate per-person costs for shared services
- **Family Dashboard**: Overview of all household subscriptions
- **Permission Levels**: Owner, editor, viewer roles
- **Cost Allocation**: Automatically split bills among users

### 4.2 Social Features
- **Public Profiles**: Share your subscription stack (optional)
- **Community Insights**: See what subscriptions are popular
- **Reviews & Ratings**: Rate and review subscription services
- **Referral Tracking**: Track referral codes and bonuses
- **Recommendation Engine**: Get suggestions based on similar users

### 6.2 Security & Privacy

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk: Feature Creep
**Mitigation**: Strict prioritization, user research validation

### Risk: Competition
**Mitigation**: Focus on unique visual approach, rapid iteration

### Risk: Monetization Challenges
**Mitigation**: Multiple revenue streams, freemium model testing

### Risk: Data Privacy Concerns
**Mitigation**: Local-first option, encryption, transparency

### Risk: Platform Dependencies
**Mitigation**: Web-first approach, progressive enhancement

## Conclusion

SubTracker has strong potential to become the go-to subscription management tool by focusing on:

1. **Visual-first approach** that makes complex data easy to understand
2. **Privacy and security** that users can trust
3. **Intelligent automation** that saves users time and money
4. **Fair pricing** that provides value at every tier
5. **Community involvement** that guides development

The roadmap is ambitious but achievable with focused execution and user feedback driving prioritization.
